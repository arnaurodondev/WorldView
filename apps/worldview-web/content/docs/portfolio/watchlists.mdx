Watchlists are named lists of instruments you actively monitor. They are separate from your brokerage holdings and can include any instrument regardless of whether you own it.

## Creating a watchlist

<Steps>
  <Step title="Go to Portfolio → Watchlists">
    Navigate to `/portfolio` and click the **Watchlists** tab.
  </Step>
  <Step title="Click + New watchlist">
    Enter a descriptive name. Suggestions: "Mag-7", "Dividend Core", "High-conviction shorts", "Earnings watch".
  </Step>
  <Step title="Add instruments">
    Type a ticker or company name in the search box. Click **+** or press Enter to add the top match.
  </Step>
</Steps>

## Managing a watchlist

- **Rename** — click the watchlist name at the top to edit it inline
- **Reorder instruments** — drag and drop rows in the watchlist table
- **Remove an instrument** — hover a row and click the trash icon
- **Bulk remove** — check multiple rows and click **Remove selected**
- **Delete watchlist** — click the ellipsis (…) menu and select **Delete**

<Callout type="warn" title="Deleting a watchlist">
Deleting a watchlist also removes all associated alert rules that target "any instrument in this watchlist." Price alert rules targeting specific tickers are not affected.
</Callout>

## Switching watchlists

Use the dropdown selector at the top of the Watchlists tab to switch between your named lists. The active watchlist also controls which instruments appear in:

- Dashboard movers widget
- Morning brief watchlist commentary
- Screener "In my watchlist" filter badge
- Alert rule targeting ("any watchlist instrument")

## Watchlist columns

| Column | Description |
|--------|-------------|
| Ticker | Symbol |
| Name | Full company or ETF name |
| Last Price | EODHD intraday last price |
| Day Change | $ and % from prior close |
| 52-week Range | Bar showing current price position |
| Market Cap | Current market capitalization |
| Added | Date you added this instrument |

<Callout type="info" title="Watchlist limits">
Free tier: 1 watchlist, 25 instruments. Pro: unlimited watchlists, 200 instruments each. Enterprise: unrestricted.
</Callout>
