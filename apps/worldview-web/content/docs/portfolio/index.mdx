The Portfolio section (`/portfolio`) combines brokerage-synced positions with your manually maintained watchlists. It gives you a single view of what you own, what you are watching, and how your capital is deployed across sectors.

## Portfolio tabs

| Tab | What it shows |
|-----|--------------|
| Holdings | Live brokerage positions with unrealized P&L |
| Transactions | Historical trade log with FIFO realized P&L |
| Watchlists | Named lists of instruments you follow |
| Allocation | Sector treemap and exposure breakdown |

## Data sources

Portfolio data comes from two places:

1. **Brokerage sync (SnapTrade)** — real positions, quantities, cost basis, and transaction history imported from your connected broker. Requires a brokerage connection. See [Connect a Brokerage](/docs/getting-started/connect-brokerage).
2. **Watchlists** — manually managed lists. No brokerage connection required.

<Callout type="info" title="No brokerage connected">
If you have not connected a brokerage, the Holdings and Transactions tabs show an empty state with a prompt to connect. Watchlists and Allocation (watchlist-based) still work fully.
</Callout>

## Portfolio metrics

The portfolio header shows aggregate metrics:

- **Total Value** — sum of all position market values at last price
- **Daily P&L ($)** — unrealized gain/loss for today
- **Daily P&L (%)** — daily P&L as a percentage of total portfolio value
- **Total Unrealized P&L** — cumulative open gain/loss across all positions
- **Day's NAV change** — portfolio-level net asset value change vs. prior close

## Morning brief integration

When you ask the AI assistant a portfolio-scoped question (e.g. "What should I watch in my portfolio this week?"), it queries your current holdings and combines them with live news context. The morning brief also references your holdings in its watchlist commentary section.
