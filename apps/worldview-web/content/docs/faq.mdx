This FAQ mirrors the [public FAQ](/#faq) on the landing page, with longer
answers for the technical questions.

## Is Worldview a real product?

Yes. It's a fully working terminal — 10 production microservices, a
Next.js frontend, and live data integrations — built as a university
final thesis demonstrating modern microservice architecture and AI
integration. You can sign up free and use it today.

## What data sources do you use?

- **EODHD** — end-of-day and intraday equities
- **Finnhub** — fundamentals + corporate events
- **SEC EDGAR** — regulatory filings
- **Polymarket** — prediction market odds
- **TastyTrade** (read-only) — brokerage sync

## Can I host Worldview myself?

The codebase is a monorepo with Docker Compose for local development. A
self-host guide is in the works; in the meantime, the [main repository
README](https://github.com/) has the setup instructions.

## How do I report a bug?

The floating feedback button in the bottom-right of any authenticated
page opens the bug-report flow. Public roadmap + voting at
[/feedback](/feedback).
