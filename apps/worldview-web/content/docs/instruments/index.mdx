An instrument page (`/instruments/:id`) is the deep-dive view for any equity, ETF, or index. It consolidates price data, fundamentals, news, entity relationships, and regulatory filings into a single five-tab layout.

## Finding an instrument

Search for any instrument using:

- `⌘K` from anywhere in the app — type ticker or company name
- The global search field in the TopBar
- The Screener — click any row to navigate to that instrument's page

## Instrument tabs

### Overview (chart)

The default tab shows a full OHLCV candlestick or line chart powered by the EODHD price series. Available controls:

- **Timeframe** — 1D, 5D, 1M, 3M, 6M, 1Y, 5Y, MAX
- **Chart type** — Candlestick, Line, Area, Bar
- **Indicators** — 7 built-in: SMA(20), SMA(50), SMA(200), EMA(12), EMA(26), RSI(14), MACD(12,26,9)
- **Drawing tools** — horizontal line, trend line, rectangle, Fibonacci retracement

### Fundamentals

Key financial metrics for equities and ETFs. See [Fundamentals](/docs/instruments/fundamentals) for the full field reference.

### News

Curated article feed for this instrument, ranked by AI relevance score. See [News](/docs/instruments/news) for routing tiers and sentiment icons.

### Intelligence

The knowledge graph showing relationships between this instrument's entity and other entities. See [Intelligence Graph](/docs/instruments/intelligence-graph).

### Filings

Recent SEC EDGAR filings: 8-K (material events), 10-K (annual report), 10-Q (quarterly report), Form 4 (insider transactions). Each filing shows a one-paragraph AI summary.

## Instrument header

Every instrument page shows a persistent header bar:

- **Ticker** and **company name**
- **Last price**, **$ change**, **% change** (updated every 60 seconds)
- **Market status** — OPEN / CLOSED / PRE-MARKET / AFTER-HOURS
- **Quick actions** — Add to watchlist, Set alert, Open in Workspace
