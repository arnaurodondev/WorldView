The morning brief is Worldview's daily AI-generated intelligence digest. It is produced once per trading day at 06:00 ET and cached until the following generation.

## Generation pipeline

The brief is produced by a multi-step RAG (Retrieval-Augmented Generation) pipeline:

1. **News retrieval** — the top 30 articles from the last 18 hours are fetched, ranked by AI relevance score and news routing tier
2. **Entity extraction** — named entities (companies, executives, regulators, macro indicators) are identified in each article
3. **Watchlist matching** — articles mentioning your watchlist instruments are flagged as high-priority
4. **Economic events** — today's macro calendar events are pulled and formatted
5. **Generation** — DeepSeek R1 Distill 32B synthesizes all context into a structured brief
6. **Citations** — every factual claim is attributed to the source article using `[N]` inline citation markers

## Brief structure

A typical morning brief follows this structure:

### Market tone
A 1-2 sentence overall read on macro conditions (risk-on/risk-off, equity vs. bond behavior, USD direction).

### Top themes
Three numbered themes dominating the news, each with a 2-3 sentence summary and citations.

### Your watchlist
Instrument-specific commentary: which holdings are in the news, what catalysts are active, and any earnings/events coming up.

### Today's calendar
Key economic releases and events happening today, with consensus vs. prior expectations.

### Research question
One forward-looking question the AI surfaces as worth investigating, based on the day's patterns.

## Interacting with the brief

**Follow-up questions** — Click **Ask a follow-up** below the brief to open the Chat panel with the brief text pre-loaded as context. You can ask "why is AAPL down?" or "what does the CPI print mean for tech?" and the AI answers with the full brief as background.

**Company links** — Company names mentioned in the brief are hyperlinked to their instrument pages. Click to open the chart directly.

**Rating** — Use the thumbs up/down widget at the bottom of the brief to rate its quality. Ratings improve future generations for your account.

<Callout type="tip" title="Portfolio questions">
To get portfolio-scoped analysis, ask the AI "What should I be watching in my portfolio this week?" in the Chat panel. It reads your current holdings from the Portfolio service and combines them with live news context.
</Callout>

## Brief freshness

| Time | Brief state |
|------|-------------|
| Before 06:00 ET | Previous day's brief shown with a "Yesterday" badge |
| 06:00–07:00 ET | New brief generating (spinner shown) |
| After 07:00 ET | Fresh brief for the current trading day |
| Weekend/holiday | Friday's brief with a "No new brief" notice |

<Callout type="info" title="Model">
The morning brief uses DeepSeek R1 Distill 32B via DeepInfra. Generation takes 8–20 seconds depending on context length. The result is cached — subsequent page loads are instant.
</Callout>
