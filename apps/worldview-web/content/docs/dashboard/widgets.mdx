Each widget on the dashboard is an independent component that fetches its own data and renders asynchronously. A widget's loading skeleton appears while data is fetching; a retry button appears if the fetch fails.

## Morning Brief

The morning brief is a 400–600 word AI-generated digest produced at 06:00 ET each trading day.

**What it covers:**
- Overall market tone (risk-on / risk-off) with macro context
- Top three news themes from the last 18 hours
- Watchlist-specific commentary: which of your holdings are in the news
- Key economic events happening today
- One actionable research question based on the day's themes

**Interaction:**
- Company names in the brief are linked to their instrument pages
- Click **Ask a follow-up** to open the Chat panel pre-loaded with the brief as context
- Rate the brief with thumbs up/down; your feedback trains the next generation

<Callout type="info" title="Watchlist scoping">
The brief is scoped to your active watchlist. Instruments not in your watchlist may still appear if they are broadly market-moving, but instrument-specific commentary focuses on your holdings.
</Callout>

## Sector Heatmap

The sector heatmap visualizes the 11 SPDR ETF sectors by percentage daily change, using a 7-step color scale:

| Color | Threshold |
|-------|-----------|
| Deep red | ≤ −2% |
| Red | −2% to −1% |
| Light red | −1% to −0.25% |
| Neutral gray | −0.25% to +0.25% |
| Light green | +0.25% to +1% |
| Green | +1% to +2% |
| Deep green | ≥ +2% |

Cell size is proportional to the sector's approximate market-cap weight in the S&P 500. Click any cell to open the Screener filtered to that sector.

## Watchlist Movers

Displays all instruments in your active watchlist sorted by absolute percentage daily change — both gainers and losers appear, enabling at-a-glance scanning of what moved most today.

Each row shows: **ticker**, **company name**, **last price**, **$ change**, **% change**, and a directional sparkline for the last 5 trading days.

## Economic Calendar

Shows the next 5 upcoming macro events with:

- **Event name** (e.g. "US CPI MoM", "FOMC Rate Decision")
- **Release time** in your local timezone
- **Consensus** estimate vs. **prior** reading
- **Actual** value (populated after release, highlighted green/red vs consensus)

Click any event to see a 12-month history of actuals vs. consensus for that indicator.

## Prediction Markets

Shows the top 5 Polymarket prediction markets by 24-hour trading volume:

- **Market title** (e.g. "Fed cuts rates in June 2026?")
- **YES probability** as a large percentage figure
- **24h volume** in USD
- **Direction arrow** showing probability change since yesterday

<Callout type="tip" title="Category filter">
Use the dropdown above the prediction markets widget to filter by category: Economics, Politics, Crypto, Sports, or All.
</Callout>
