Worldview integrates with real brokerage accounts via **SnapTrade**, a read-only aggregation layer. Your positions appear in the Portfolio section and the morning brief can reference your actual holdings when answering research questions.

## Supported brokers

Currently supported through SnapTrade:

- **TastyTrade** — equity and options positions
- More brokers coming via SnapTrade's growing catalog

<Callout type="warn" title="Read-only access only">
Worldview requests a read-only OAuth token. It cannot place orders, cancel trades, transfer funds, or modify your brokerage account in any way. The SnapTrade connection is scoped to position and transaction data only.
</Callout>

## Connect TastyTrade

<Steps>
  <Step title="Open Settings">
    Click the gear icon in the top-right of the TopBar, or navigate to [/settings](/settings).
  </Step>
  <Step title="Go to Brokerage">
    Select **Brokerage** from the Settings sidebar.
  </Step>
  <Step title="Click Connect TastyTrade">
    Click the **Connect TastyTrade** button. You are redirected to SnapTrade's connection portal.
  </Step>
  <Step title="Authenticate with TastyTrade">
    Enter your TastyTrade credentials in SnapTrade's secure portal. Worldview never sees your brokerage password.
  </Step>
  <Step title="Authorize read-only scope">
    SnapTrade asks you to approve read-only access. Confirm the scope and click **Authorize**.
  </Step>
  <Step title="Return to Worldview">
    You are redirected back to Settings. Worldview begins syncing your positions — the first sync takes up to 30 seconds.
  </Step>
</Steps>

## What syncs

After connection, Worldview imports:

| Data type | Update frequency |
|-----------|-----------------|
| Current positions (holdings) | Every 5 minutes |
| Transaction history | On first sync + daily |
| Account balances | Every 5 minutes |

<Callout type="info" title="Historical transactions">
Worldview imports up to 12 months of transaction history on the first sync. FIFO realized P&L is calculated from this history. Older transactions show as "position established before sync date."
</Callout>

## Disconnect a brokerage

Navigate to **Settings → Brokerage** and click **Disconnect**. Worldview deletes the SnapTrade token and removes synced position data. Transaction history is retained for accounting purposes unless you explicitly delete your account.

## Troubleshooting

**Positions not appearing after 2 minutes** — The initial sync may be delayed. Refresh the Portfolio page. If the issue persists, disconnect and reconnect.

**Authorization failed** — Your TastyTrade password may have changed since connecting. Disconnect and reconnect with your current credentials.

**Missing instruments** — Worldview requires EODHD market data coverage for an instrument to appear in Portfolio. Most US equities and ETFs are covered; options positions show the underlying instrument.
