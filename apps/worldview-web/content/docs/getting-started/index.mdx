This guide gets you from a fresh sign-up to a working Worldview workspace with a watchlist, a chart, and the AI assistant. Total time: ~5 minutes.

## Before you begin

You need:

- A web browser (Chrome, Safari, Firefox, or Edge — all current)
- An email address
- *(Optional)* A TastyTrade brokerage account if you want to sync positions

## Sign up

<Steps>
  <Step title="Create an account">
    Visit [the registration page](/register) and enter your email + password. We don't ask for a credit card.
  </Step>
  <Step title="Verify your email">
    Click the verification link in your inbox. Worldview's auth runs through Zitadel — the link expires in 24h.
  </Step>
  <Step title="Land on the dashboard">
    First sign-in lands you on `/dashboard`. The morning brief, watchlists, and AI panel are visible by default.
  </Step>
</Steps>

## Build your first watchlist

The watchlist is the central focus list — every alert, brief, and notification scopes by it.

<Callout type="info" title="Watchlist limits">
The free tier allows 1 watchlist with up to 25 instruments. Pro and Enterprise are unlimited.
</Callout>

1. Click **Watchlists** in the left rail
2. Click **+ New watchlist**, name it (e.g. "Tech mega-cap")
3. Search for an instrument (try `AAPL`) and click **Add**
4. Repeat for any tickers you actively follow

## Connect your brokerage (optional)

Worldview reads brokerage positions to show actual P&L next to research. Currently we support TastyTrade.

<Callout type="warn" title="Read-only access">
Brokerage tokens are scoped read-only — Worldview cannot place orders, transfer cash, or modify your account.
</Callout>

Settings → Brokerage → **Connect TastyTrade**. Follow the OAuth flow; positions sync within ~30 seconds.
