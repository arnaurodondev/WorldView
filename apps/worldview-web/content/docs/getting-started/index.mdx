This guide gets you from a fresh sign-up to a working Worldview workspace with a watchlist, a chart, and the AI assistant. Total time: approximately five minutes.

## Before you begin

You need:

- A web browser (Chrome, Safari, Firefox, or Edge — all current versions)
- An email address
- *(Optional)* A TastyTrade brokerage account if you want to sync your positions

## Sign up

<Steps>
  <Step title="Create an account">
    Visit the [registration page](/register) and enter your email and password. No credit card required.
  </Step>
  <Step title="Verify your email">
    Click the verification link in your inbox. Authentication runs through Zitadel — the link expires in 24 hours.
  </Step>
  <Step title="Land on the dashboard">
    Your first sign-in lands you on `/dashboard`. The morning brief, watchlists, and AI panel are visible by default.
  </Step>
</Steps>

<Callout type="tip" title="Development login">
When running Worldview locally, visit `/login` and click the **Dev Login** button to skip the full OIDC flow and receive a test JWT instantly.
</Callout>

## Build your first watchlist

The watchlist is the central focus list — every alert, morning brief, and notification scopes by it.

<Callout type="info" title="Watchlist limits">
The free tier allows 1 watchlist with up to 25 instruments. Pro and Enterprise are unlimited.
</Callout>

1. Click **Watchlists** in the left navigation rail
2. Click **+ New watchlist**, give it a name (e.g. "Tech mega-cap")
3. Search for an instrument (try `AAPL`) and click **Add**
4. Repeat for any tickers you actively follow

## Connect your brokerage (optional)

Worldview reads brokerage positions to show actual P&L alongside your research. Currently supported: TastyTrade.

<Callout type="warn" title="Read-only access">
Brokerage tokens are scoped read-only — Worldview cannot place orders, transfer cash, or modify your account in any way.
</Callout>

Navigate to **Settings → Brokerage → Connect TastyTrade**. Complete the OAuth flow; your positions sync within approximately 30 seconds.

## Explore the workspace

The workspace gives you a multi-panel canvas where you can pin charts, news feeds, fundamentals panels, and more side-by-side. Press `G` then `W` to jump directly to the workspace from anywhere in the app.
