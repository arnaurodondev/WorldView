Worldview is an institutional-grade market intelligence terminal. This documentation covers everything from your first sign-up through advanced API usage and the entity knowledge graph.

## Getting started

Start with the [getting-started guide](/docs/getting-started) — it walks you through sign-up, your first watchlist, and a quick tour of the workspace.

If you're a developer, the [API reference](/docs/api-reference) documents every endpoint exposed by the S9 gateway.

## What you can do

- **Screen** 8,000+ instruments by fundamentals, technicals, news velocity, or market impact
- **Track** entity relationships in an interactive knowledge graph (companies, executives, suppliers, regulators)
- **Get notified** with rule-based alerts on price, technical levels, fundamental changes, or news
- **Ask** the AI research assistant — answers cite the article, filing, or paragraph they came from
- **Sync** your brokerage to track P&L alongside your research

<Callout type="tip" title="Keyboard-first">
Press <kbd>⌘K</kbd> to open the search command palette from anywhere in the docs or the app.
</Callout>

## Architecture

Worldview is built on 10 microservices, 6 shared Python libraries, and a Next.js 15 frontend. The full architecture is documented in [docs/MASTER_PLAN.md](https://github.com/) (link to repo).
