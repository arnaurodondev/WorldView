## Open knobs (very small)

- FU-3.3: name the new metric `rag_answer_quality` or extend `rag_citation_accuracy`? Recommendation: new metric — different cardinality (per-answer vs per-citation).
- FU-3.4: should `[BRF]` hovercard show the full brief or just `headline + lead`? Recommendation: `headline + lead` (matches `<MessageMetaStrip>` density floor); user clicks through to brief surface for full content.
- FU-3.6: tooltip timezone — UTC always, or user's local? Recommendation: UTC always (terminal convention; ambiguity-free across timezones).
